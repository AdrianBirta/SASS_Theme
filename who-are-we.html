Who are we?

KOALA.CH is the first online shop in Switzerland with free shipping and free return. We have launched this shoe and fashion accessories shop in November 2010, with the ambition to offer a large selection of products with a top-of-class customer service. After a smooth launch, we quickly saw that there was a great demand for online shoes, and therefore pushed hard to make sure we could offer more products in the best brands that Swiss people love. Most customers come to KOALA.CH because they don't find what they want in their area, others are just fed-up with going to the city center or the mall (even worse with kids...) when they can just simply order from us and try their shoes at home without any risks.

More than
300 Shoe Brands

With only a handful of brands in the beginning, we now offer over a hundred elegant, lifestyle or sports brands. The most famous are UGG, Timberland, Geox, Converse, Nike, Sorel or Paul Green but we also offer more confidential brands such as Andrea Puccini high heels, Veja sneakers or kids shoes from Robeez.

Your Boutique
= Your Style

Since the spring of 2012, we propose a different way to navigate our large selection, with specialized boutiques. Visit for example our 100% Chic Boutique for a chic and glamourous look, the top shoe trends for summer in our Vamos a la Playa Boutique, or our Green Mind Boutique for a large selection of ecological and fair trade shoes.

Since the summer of 2014, we have joined Chaussures Aeschbach in Geneva and have been able since to continue to grow the assortment and even offer better prices. We are lucky to have our own warehouse in the countryside, and a rather cool office for our team. Our modest team sizes lets us really focus on what's essential: customer satisfaction! We do this thanks to a very local and personalized approach, thanks to people. As we always want to offer the best service, we are always pleased to receive feedback. So don't hesitate if you have comments or questions, or just want to chat... we are reachable on feedback@koala.ch.
